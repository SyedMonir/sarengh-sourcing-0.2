Corporate

Leadership

George Ling

Founder and Chairman

Born and raised in Shanghai, China. Moved to Hong Kong in 1949 and began working in international trade. Founded Sarengh in 1960 and incorporated the company in Hong Kong in 1971. Serves as Chairman of the Board of Sarengh Group.

Michael Tsai

Group Executive Director

Michael was born in Taiwan and raised in California. Michael graduated with a degree in Computer Science from University of California, Berkeley. Michael joined Sarengh Group in 2001 and chairs the Management Board.

Alex Angelchik

Group Managing Director

Alex was born and raised in the United States. He holds a bachelors degree in Business Finance and well as a masters degree in International Marketing. Alex joined Sarengh in 2006 and has over 25 years of apparel sourcing experience. In addition to managing the companies business units, Alex serves as Board of Directors Advisor and sits on the Management Board.
