SWEATERS

Sarengh specialize in sweater gauges 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 14, 16 and 18.

We have vast experience in a wide variety of yarns, including silks, linens, wools and cashmere.

We are also experts in sweaters trims andinnovative wash/print techniques.

CUT AND SEWN KNIT

Sarengh offers competitive costs combined with excellent quality from a variety of countries acrossEurope, Asia and the Middle East.

.

Our knit capabilities extend from basics to fashion items, casual to formal, across all wash, finish and fabric combinations.

DENIM PRODUCTS

Denim is one of Sarengh strongest categories, led by some of the best talent in Asia within our fabric and finish development teams.

Execution is supported by our highly experienced and dedicated on-site wet and dry processing teams.

CAREER WEAR

Sarengh specializes in highly constructed garments; each style carefully crafted and expertly fitted by our highly skilled technical and pattern engineers.

We have exceptional production covering all key categories including tailored suites, jackets, dresses, outerwear, shirts and formal pants.

CASUAL WOVENS

Whether its pants, tops or outerwear, Sarengh offers truly excellent packages from our many countries of origin.

Our design, fabric and wash teams can really offer your company amazing services and products.

PERFORMANCE SPORTS APPAREL

Sarengh is at the forefront of some of the mosttechnologically advanced fabrics and finishes in the industry. Our in-country Korean and Taiwanese fabric teams are on the ground to provide exceptional support and service.
